Find answers to common questions about Trash to Treasure — from getting started to earning rewards.

Getting Started

Trash to Treasure (T2T) is a university recycling reward system. Students deposit plastic bottles into smart machines on campus to earn eco-points, redeemable for food vouchers, merchandise, and more.

Download the T2T mobile app and sign up with your university email and student ID. Once your department admin approves your account, you can start depositing bottles immediately.

Any enrolled student can register via the mobile app. Admin access is granted only to department administrators and super users by the IT team.

Yes! Download the T2T Student App on Google Play or the App Store. There is also a T2T Admin App for department administrators.
Recycling Guide

PET plastic bottles (codes 1 & 2) up to 1.5 litres. Bottles must be empty, clean, and have the cap removed. Glass, cans, and non-plastic containers are not accepted.

Common reasons: bottle not empty, cap still on, bottle is crushed, or the machine is near full capacity. Try another machine or clean the bottle first.

Machines are monitored in real time. Staff are notified at 80% capacity. Most machines are serviced within 24 hours of reaching full capacity.
Points & Rewards

The default rate is 5 points per bottle. Department admins can set a custom rate. Check your department's rate in the T2T app.

T2T Student App → Rewards → select a reward → tap Redeem. You'll receive a ticket code to present to your department admin.

Points do not currently expire. Redeemed points are deducted from your balance, but your total lifetime bottle count is preserved for leaderboards.

Redemptions need approval from your department admin. Once the ticket is verified, the status changes to Completed and your reward is ready to collect.
Machines

Machine locations can be viewed inside the T2T Student App under the Map section. Machines are placed at various campus buildings and common areas.

Try a nearby machine. You can also report the issue so our team can investigate promptly.

Check your Transactions in the app. If nothing shows, submit a report with the machine ID, date, and time.
Account & Login

In the mobile app, tap Forgot Password on the login screen. A reset link will be sent to your registered email. Admin accounts should contact the Super User.

Accounts lock after 5 failed attempts. Wait 30 minutes, or contact us to unlock it immediately.

Students: open the T2T app → Profile → Edit. For department changes, contact us with your student ID and new department.
